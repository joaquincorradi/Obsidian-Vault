# Integrales de línea
- Diferencial de arco: $dlc=ds=\|\dot{\vec{r(t)}}dt\|$

Calcular $\vec{r}(t)$:
$$
\begin{align}
\int_{C}f(x,y)ds&=\int_{a}^b f[x(t),y(t)]\|\dot{\vec{r}}(t)\|dt \\
&=\int_{a}^b (f\circ \vec{r})(t)\|\dot{\vec{r}}(t)\|dt \\
\int_{C}f(x,y,z)ds&=\int_{a}^b f[x(t),y(t),z(t)]\|\dot{\vec{r}}(t)\|dt
\end{align}
$$
